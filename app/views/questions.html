Questions

Q: What is included in my lot fee?
A: Curbside trash pick up is included in your lot fee. All real estate taxes and all utilities are paid by the resident.

Q: What type of amenities does Rolling Acres Estates have?
A: Rolling Acres Estates has many amenities including paved streets, RV storage, swimming pool, playground area, club house, on site courtesy officer, off street parking and ample visitor parking.

Q: Can I landscape around my home?
A: Residents are permitted to landscape around their home, but they are required to get approval by the management before any changes are made.

Q: Can I store my boat or RV at my lot?
A: Rolling Acres does not allow residents to store their recreational vehicles at their lot, but we do have a separate secured area where your boat and RV can be stored.

Q: What are the educational opportunities near Rolling Acres Estates?
A: We are located in the award winning Southwest Local School District (please see links for more information). Plus we are close to facilities where you can further your education, such as Cincinnati State, University of Cincinnati and the College of Mount St. Joseph.

Q: Are pets allowed?
A: Residents are allowed to have a small indoor pet. Other restrictions my apply.
